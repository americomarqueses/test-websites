ℹ️ About GCP Environments

GCP environments are isolated cloud environments for your project. Each environment follows the naming convention:

  • DEV: Development environment for testing and development
    Format: airasia-[project-name]-dev
  • STG: Staging environment for pre-production testing
    Format: airasia-[project-name]-stg
  • PRD: Production environment for live applications
    Format: airasia-[project-name]-prd

Service Accounts

Service accounts are used for applications and services to authenticate and access GCP resources.

Naming Convention
DEV: sa-dev
STG: sa-stg
PRD: sa-prd
Use lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only. Maximum 30 characters.
Service Account Email Preview
